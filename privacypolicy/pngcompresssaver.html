PNG Compress Saver

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2023/12/11

This Privacy Policy outlines how PNG Compress Saver ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide while using PNG Compress Saver, a Chrome extension designed to download and compress PNG images from web pages.

Information Collection and Use

PNG Compress Saver is designed to operate without collecting any personal data from its users. The extension does not require users to create an account, nor does it collect personal identifiers, financial information, health information, or other sensitive data. The following outlines the extent of data interaction by PNG Compress Saver:
- Image Processing: PNG Compress Saver accesses images only based on user-initiated actions. It does not store these images or any associated data.
- No Data Storage: The extension does not store any data on our servers. All operations are performed locally on the user's device.
- No Data Sharing: We do not share any data with third parties, as we do not collect any data.

Permissions Used

PNG Compress Saver requires certain permissions to function effectively, which are solely for the operation of the extension:
- activeTab: To interact with images on the web pages you visit. - downloads: To save images to your device. - scripting: To inject scripts for image processing. - storage: To save your settings and preferences locally on your device.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. As PNG Compress Saver does not collect, store, or transmit personal data, the risk of data breach is significantly minimized.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
